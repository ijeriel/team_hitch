Description of Attributes

  • Dec: Final decision
  • Match: yes / no
  • Like: How much you like that person
  • Attr: Rating of the subject
  • Dec_0: Decision of the night of event
  • Prob: How probable do you think it is that this person will say 'yes' for you?
  • Sinc: how important you think sincere is for that person
  • Attr2_1: What do you think the opposite sex looks for in a date
  • Attr1_1: importance of the attribute
