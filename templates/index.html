Speed Dating - ML Model

Problem Statement:
The purpose of this project to see if single people can meet their prospective partner in a short amount time.
The process of speed dating involves a face-to-face session that last approximately 4 minutes. The formalized
process encourages the individuals to meet a large number of potential partners in a short period of time. During
the “date” the participants discuss whatever they want. Ideally, speed dating is based the notion that you can
tell if you are interested or not within minutes.

The data was compiled from a study done at Columbia University and retrieved from Kaggle.
